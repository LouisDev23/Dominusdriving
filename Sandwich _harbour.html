Sandwich Harbour is a natural wonder on the Atlantic coast of Namibia, where towering dunes meet the ocean, and a lagoon teems with birdlife. It is a place of history, beauty, and adventure that you can explore on a guided tour with us.

Reasons to Join Us on this Unforgettable Trip:

  • You will experience the thrill of a 4x4 drive through the Namib Desert, where you will see stunning landscapes, wildlife, and archaeological sites.
  • You will witness the amazing spectacle of Sandwich Harbour, where dunes up to 100m-high plunge into the Atlantic, creating a dramatic contrast of colours and shapes. You will have the opportunity to take stunning photos and videos of this unique scenery.
  • You will enjoy a delicious champagne and a platter of local delicacies upon arrival at Sandwich Harbour, while admiring the view and relaxing in the fresh breeze. You will also have the chance to explore the dunes, or simply soak in the atmosphere of this serene and secluded spot.
  • You will have a professional and friendly guide who will ensure your safety and comfort throughout the tour. You will also have access to a comfortable and well-equipped 4x4 vehicle, which will take you to and from Sandwich Harbour.

If you are looking for a tour that combines adventure, nature, and history, then Sandwich Harbour is the perfect destination for you.
